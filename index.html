About JonesCoin

The future of transportation industry payments

JonesCoin is a specialized cryptocurrency built on a secure blockchain foundation, designed specifically for the transportation industry. Our mission is to simplify and streamline payment processes for carriers, shippers, and all stakeholders in the logistics ecosystem.

With JonesCoin, transactions that traditionally took days can be completed in seconds, reducing administrative overhead and improving cash flow for transportation businesses of all sizes.

Industry Use Cases

How JonesCoin is transforming transportation payments

Fuel Payments

Drivers can pay for fuel instantly at participating stations using JonesCoin, eliminating the need for fuel cards and reducing fraud. Fleet managers can allocate funds in real-time based on route requirements.

Learn more

Lumper Fees

No more waiting for lumper fee approvals or cash advances. Drivers can pay lumper fees directly with JonesCoin, with automatic documentation and receipt generation for accounting purposes.

Learn more

Detention Pay

Smart contracts automatically calculate and process detention payments based on GPS data and electronic logging device information, ensuring fair compensation without disputes.

Freight Payments
